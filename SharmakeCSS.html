Data Science.

First, I will define the meaning of the term Data science. Data science is the field of applying advanced analytics techniques and scientific principles to extract valuable information from data for business decision-making, strategic planning and other uses. In other words, the job of Data Scientist is to analyze and write sift down mega sized data into more reasonable and understandable information for the company or business to make a more precise decision.

Here are some of Data Scientist's objectives:

  • Solve real company problems using data analysis
  • Provide ways to advance data management tools for everyone
  • Focus more on replication than production
  • Solve real company problems using data analysis

Here are the tools you need:

  • Algorithms, programming languages (R, Python, SQL, etc.), formulas, math, Teamwork, Communication, Honesty and Trust.

Here are of the possible job roles/positions:

  • Data analyst, Business Analyst, Database Administrator, Data Engineer, Data Scientist, Data and Analytics Manager, and Statistician.
